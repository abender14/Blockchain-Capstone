Our Mission

Due to the huge inflow of credit disadvantaged and credit invisible population, the P2P lending market space has been growing rapidly for the past decade and reached over $40 billion in 2018. However, borrowers are still facing high interest rates and investors are also carrying high credit risk.

Appearo is working beyond the conventional credit risk assessment in order to bring security, equity, and transparency to the P2P lending market.
