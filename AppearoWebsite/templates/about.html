About Us

Our Company

Starting as an Industrial Engineering capstone project at Northeastern University, Appearo was built by four emerging engineers who saw an opportunity to improve access to capital through an alternative creditworthiness assessment and a blockchain platform. After a year of development and iteration, Appearo has built a platform that strives to increase security, equity, and transparency in credit lending.

Our Co-Founders

Alex Bender has worked to develop Appearo's predictive machine learning algorithms, user-interface, and Blockchain platform, among other things. Prior to co-founding Appearo, Alex served as a Project Manager in Clinical Process Improvement at Lahey Health System, a Mergers & Acquisitions Management Consultant at PricewaterhouseCoopers, and an Engineering & Finance Analyst at Boom Supersonic, which is a start-up supersonic aircraft manufacturer.
