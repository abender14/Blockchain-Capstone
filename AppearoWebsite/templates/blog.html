Our Blockchain Network

Blockchain is a distributed data system that allows people to lend and borrow at scale without a centralized intermediary such as a bank.



Peer to Peer

Appearo is a peer to peer platform that enables you to share/transact with anyone else on the network. Blockchain stores data across a network (decentralized).

Transparency

Appearo is a transparent platform. Unlike Banks that give you a limited view, Appearo stores all of your transactions across the network.

Security

Appearo is a secure transaction platform. Because your data is distributed across the network, it cannot be hacked.





Appearo uses it's blockchain network as the transaction platform/database
